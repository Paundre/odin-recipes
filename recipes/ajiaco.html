Ajiaco

Ajiaco is one of the many regional Colombian dishes you can’t leave the country without trying. It’s a hearty soup made from three different kinds of potatoes, chicken, guasca leaves, with a half an ear of corn splashed in for good measure. Most times, Ajiaco is accompanied by a plate of rice and a slice of avocado.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Water
  • Corn
  • Salt and pepper
  • Onion
  • Garlic head
  • Cilantro
  • Potatoes
  • Guasca leaves
  • Rice
  • Avocado
