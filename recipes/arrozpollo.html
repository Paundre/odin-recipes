Arroz con pollo

Arroz con Pollo is a true classic of Colombian gastronomy. I don't think there are two ingredients that go together as well as chicken and rice. It is an easy recipe to prepare, tasty and yielding.

Ingredients

  • Cooked chicken breast
  • Oil
  • Water
  • Salt and pepper
  • Rice
  • Garlic head
  • Onion
  • Carrot
  • Peas
  • Kidney beans
  • Bell peppers

Steps

  1. Place the oil in a large pot over low heat. Saut√© the chopped peppers, garlic and onion and stir until they take on a golden color.
  2. Add the rice an stir well.
  3. Add the cups of water, the diced vegetables, the chicken breasts and salt to taste. Stir and let cook over medium heat for approximately 15 minutes or until the rice is dry.
  4. Once the rice has absorbed all the water, cover the pot and cook over low heat for 10 to 15 more minutes until the rice is soft.
